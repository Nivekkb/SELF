## Preamble

By accessing SELF documentation in the buyers folder, you ("Buyer" or "You") acknowledge and agree to the following governance terms. These terms establish the framework for responsible engagement with SELF's intellectual property, safety documentation, and governance materials.

This is not a legal license but a governance agreement that ensures documentation access aligns with SELF's mission of safe, ethical, and beneficial AI development.

---

## Acceptance of Governance Principles

### 1. Safety-First Commitment
You acknowledge that AI safety is paramount and agree to:
- Treat all safety documentation as immutable and authoritative
- Not attempt to circumvent or weaken safety mechanisms
- Support the development of beneficial AI systems
- Report any safety concerns immediately to the appropriate authorities

### 2. Responsible Use Agreement
You commit to using SELF documentation responsibly by:
- Maintaining confidentiality of sensitive information
- Using acquired knowledge for beneficial purposes only
- Respecting intellectual property rights and attribution requirements
- Complying with all applicable laws and regulations

### 3. Ethical Engagement Pledge
You pledge to engage ethically by:
- Conducting due diligence with integrity and transparency
- Avoiding manipulative or coercive acquisition practices
- Respecting the autonomy and values of all stakeholders
- Contributing positively to the AI safety ecosystem

### 4. Documentation Integrity Agreement
You agree to maintain documentation integrity by:
- Not modifying, altering, or misrepresenting SELF documentation
- Properly attributing sources when referencing materials
- Respecting the hierarchical structure of safety documentation
- Following established change management processes

---

## Access Conditions

### Documentation Access Rights
Subject to your acceptance of these terms, you are granted access to:
- Public SELF documentation and specifications
- Safety and governance frameworks
- Technical implementation guidance
- Due diligence and evaluation materials

### Access Restrictions
You expressly agree not to:
- Share restricted documentation without authorization
- Use documentation for competitive intelligence gathering
- Attempt to reverse-engineer proprietary safety mechanisms
- Distribute documentation in violation of these terms

---

## Governance Obligations

### Continuous Compliance
You agree to maintain ongoing compliance by:
- Staying informed of documentation updates
- Participating in governance feedback processes
- Respecting evolving safety standards
- Supporting transparency and accountability measures

### Reporting Requirements
You commit to reporting:
- Any suspected safety violations or concerns
- Potential misuse of SELF technology or documentation
- Conflicts of interest in acquisition processes
- Material changes in your risk profile or intentions

---

## Dispute Resolution

### Governance Disputes
Any disputes regarding documentation interpretation shall be resolved through:
1. Initial consultation with the Documentation Council
2. Mediation by neutral AI safety experts
3. Final determination by the Safety Documentation Council

### Termination Rights
Either party may terminate this governance agreement with cause, including:
- Material breach of safety principles
- Violation of responsible use commitments
- Failure to maintain ethical engagement standards
