# Buyer Reading Order

## Due Diligence and Acquisition Evaluation

**Total Time**: 4-5 hours | **Documents**: 5 | **Purpose**: Informed acquisition decision-making

### Phase 0: Governance Acceptance (15 min)
1. **GOVERNANCE_ACCEPTANCE_CLAUSE.md** (15 min)
   - *Why*: Establish governance terms for accessing SELF buyer documentation
   - *Key Takeaways*: Safety commitments, responsible use, ethical engagement
   - *Decision Impact*: Confirm alignment with SELF governance principles
   - *Note*: Must be accepted before proceeding with other documentation

### Phase 1: Self-Assessment (1 hour)
2. **packages/self-engine/SELF_DOCS/buyers/BUYER_ALIGNMENT_ASSESSMENT.md** (60 min)
   - *Why*: Comprehensive evaluation of organizational alignment with SELF principles
   - *Key Takeaways*: Qualification level, improvement areas, partnership readiness
   - *Decision Impact*: Determine if organization is ready for SELF partnership

### Phase 2: Protection and Terms (2 hours)
3. **packages/self-engine/SELF_DOCS/buyers/BUYER-PROTECTION-EXPLAINER.md** (45 min)
   - *Why*: Understand how escrow and guarantees protect buyers
   - *Key Takeaways*: Buyer protections, escrow mechanisms, risk mitigation
   - *Decision Impact*: Assess investment security and downside protection

4. **packages/self-engine/SELF_DOCS/buyers/BUY-SELL-AGREEMENT-ESCROW.md** (60 min)
   - *Why*: Complete acquisition terms and conditions
   - *Key Takeaways*: Pricing tiers, escrow framework, clawback provisions
   - *Decision Impact*: Understand legal terms and financial commitments

### Phase 3: Evaluation Criteria (1 hour)
5. **packages/self-engine/SELF_DOCS/buyers/VALUES-FIRST-SALES-CRITERIA.md** (45 min)
   - *Why*: Qualification requirements for SELF acquisition
   - *Key Takeaways*: Evaluation criteria, approval process, mission alignment
   - *Decision Impact*: Understand formal qualification process

**Next Steps**: If assessment shows readiness, review docs/executives/EXECUTIVE-BRIEF.md for strategic framework.
