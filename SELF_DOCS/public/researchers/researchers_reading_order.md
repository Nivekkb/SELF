## Research Methodology Guidelines

### Critical Analysis Framework
When reading each document, apply these research lenses:

1. **Philosophical Analysis**: Examine underlying assumptions and ethical foundations
2. **Technical Feasibility**: Assess implementation practicality and scalability
3. **Empirical Validation**: Evaluate evidence quality and completeness
4. **Comparative Analysis**: Compare with alternative approaches in AI safety
5. **Future Implications**: Consider long-term consequences and research directions

### Research Note-Taking Template
For each document, maintain notes on:

- **Key Claims**: What does the document claim?
- **Evidence Quality**: How well are claims supported?
- **Assumptions**: What underlying assumptions are made?
- **Limitations**: What are the acknowledged and unacknowledged limitations?
- **Research Gaps**: What questions remain unanswered?
- **Connections**: How does this relate to broader AI safety research?

### Collaborative Research Approach
- **Peer Review**: Share analyses with other researchers
- **Interdisciplinary Dialogue**: Connect with ethicists, computer scientists, policymakers
- **Open Questions**: Maintain a list of research questions raised by the documentation
- **Contribution Opportunities**: Identify areas where you can contribute to the framework
