# SELF Regulator Briefing

**Date**: December 22, 2025  
**Audience**: Regulatory Agencies, Policy Makers, Government Officials  
**Classification**: Public

## Executive Summary

SELF (Support-First Logic Engine) is a constitutional AI safety framework designed specifically for emotional AI applications. 
It provides comprehensive safety guarantees while intentionally avoiding medical device classification and other healthcare regulations. 
SELF represents a proactive approach to AI safety that exceeds current regulatory requirements.

## What is SELF?

### Core Definition
SELF is a **constitutional AI safety framework** that acts as a middleware safety layer between AI models and user interfaces. It enforces 
immutable safety principles through self-governance mechanisms.

### Key Characteristics
- **Non-Medical**: Intentionally designed to avoid medical device classification
- **Safety-First**: Prioritizes user safety over functionality
- **Constitutional**: Uses immutable safety doctrines that cannot be overridden
- **Transparent**: Provides comprehensive documentation and audit trails

## Regulatory Positioning

### What SELF Is NOT
- ❌ **Medical Device**: No diagnostic, treatment, or prevention capabilities
- ❌ **Clinical Decision Support**: No clinical recommendations or treatment decisions
- ❌ **Therapeutic System**: No therapy or treatment protocols
- ❌ **Diagnostic Tool**: No medical or psychological condition identification

### Why This Matters
SELF's intentional non-medical positioning allows for:
- **Faster Innovation**: Avoids lengthy medical device approval processes
- **Broader Access**: Enables wider deployment without healthcare licensing
- **Safety Enhancement**: Provides safety measures beyond current requirements
- **Regulatory Clarity**: Clear boundaries prevent regulatory ambiguity

## Safety Framework

### Constitutional Governance
SELF operates under immutable safety principles:
- **Hard Invariants**: Cannot be violated under any circumstances
- **Self-Correction**: Automatic detection and correction of safety violations
- **Human Oversight**: Explicit human control and intervention mechanisms
- **Exit Protocols**: Safe disengagement procedures for all scenarios

### Key Safety Features
- **Override Prevention**: Blocks all attempts to bypass safety constraints
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Continuous safety validation during operation
- **Audit Trails**: Complete logging of all safety decisions and interventions
- **Uncertainty Acknowledgment**: Explicit recognition of system limitations

## Regulatory Compliance

### Privacy Regulations (FULL COMPLIANCE ✅)
- **GDPR**: Exceeds European privacy requirements
- **CCPA**: Full California privacy compliance
- **PIPEDA**: Canadian privacy protection standards
- **Data Minimization**: Collects only essential safety-related data

### AI-Specific Frameworks
- **NIST AI RMF**: Strong alignment with governance and risk management
- **EU AI Act**: Conditional compliance for emotional AI applications
- **IEEE Standards**: Full alignment with ethical AI design principles

### What We Avoid
- **HIPAA**: Not applicable - no protected health information handling
- **FDA Regulations**: Intentionally avoided through non-medical design
- **Medical Device Licensing**: Clear functional boundaries prevent classification
