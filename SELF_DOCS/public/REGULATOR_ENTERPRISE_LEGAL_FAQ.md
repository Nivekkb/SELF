## General Questions

### Q1: What exactly is SELF?

**A**: SELF (Support-First Logic Engine) is a constitutional AI safety framework that acts as a middleware safety layer 
between AI models and user interfaces. It enforces immutable safety principles through self-governance mechanisms specifically designed for emotional AI applications.

**Key Points**:
- Not an AI model itself, but a safety framework
- Constitutional approach with immutable safety doctrines
- Focuses on emotional AI and mental health support contexts
- Provides safety guarantees that exceed regulatory requirements

### Q2: Is SELF a medical device?

**A**: **No.** SELF is intentionally designed to avoid medical device classification under FDA regulations and similar frameworks worldwide.

**Why This Matters**:
- No diagnostic capabilities
- No treatment or prevention functions
- No therapeutic interventions
- No clinical decision-making

**Regulatory Impact**: This positioning allows for faster deployment and broader access while maintaining safety standards.

### Q3: Does SELF provide therapy or treatment?

**A**: **No.** SELF provides emotional support and safety monitoring but does not offer therapy, treatment, or clinical interventions.

**What SELF Does Provide**:
- Safety boundary enforcement
- Emotional state detection for safety purposes
- Crisis intervention protocols
- Supportive conversation guidance

**What SELF Does NOT Provide**:
- Therapy sessions
- Treatment plans
- Clinical recommendations
- Medical advice
