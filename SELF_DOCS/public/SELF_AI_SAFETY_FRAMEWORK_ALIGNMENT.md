# SELF Alignment with Recognized AI Safety Frameworks

## Executive Summary

This document provides an honest assessment of SELF's alignment with recognized AI safety frameworks and standards. 
SELF demonstrates strong alignment with several established frameworks while maintaining its unique constitutional approach to emotional AI safety.

## Framework Alignment Assessment

### 1. Constitutional AI Frameworks

#### Alignment: **STRONG** ✅

**SELF demonstrates strong alignment with Constitutional AI principles:**

- **Immutable Core Rules**: Like Anthropic's Constitutional AI, SELF implements immutable safety doctrines that cannot be overridden
- **Self-Governance**: Both frameworks emphasize AI systems that can self-correct and maintain safety boundaries
- **Principle-Based Constraints**: Both use high-level principles to guide AI behavior rather than exhaustive rule lists

**Key Similarities:**
- Hard invariants that cannot be violated
- Self-monitoring and correction mechanisms
- Constitutional documents that serve as supreme governing authority

**Key Differences:**
- SELF focuses specifically on emotional support and mental health contexts
- SELF includes explicit human oversight integration
- SELF implements more granular exit decision frameworks
