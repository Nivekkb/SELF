# Business Reading Order

## Strategic Business Considerations and Market Positioning

**Total Time**: 2-3 hours | **Documents**: 6 | **Purpose**: Business strategy and market assessment

### Phase 1: Market Positioning (1 hour)
1. **SELF-PRICING.md** (30 min)
   - *Why*: Pricing strategy and business model
   - *Key Takeaways*: Revenue models, pricing tiers, value proposition
   - *Business Impact*: Understand monetization strategy

2. **WHY_GOVERNANCE_COSTS_MONEY.md** (15 min)
   - *Why*: Explains what governance is (and why it’s funded)
   - *Key Takeaways*: Auditability, monotonic safety, enforcement against drift
   - *Business Impact*: Align expectations on non-negotiable safety operations

3. **SELF-NOT-FOR-EVERYONE.md** (30 min)
   - *Why*: Target market definition and boundaries
   - *Key Takeaways*: Who should use SELF, who should not, market segmentation
   - *Business Impact*: Customer qualification and market focus

### Phase 2: Risk and Assurance (1-2 hours)
4. **SELF-NOT-FOR-YOU.md** (30 min)
   - *Why*: Specific contraindications and risk factors
   - *Key Takeaways*: High-risk use cases, danger signs, liability considerations
   - *Business Impact*: Risk management and liability mitigation

5. **SELF-KEY-GENERATION.md** (30 min)
   - *Why*: Key generation and distribution mechanisms
   - *Key Takeaways*: Security models, key management, distribution protocols
   - *Business Impact*: Security assurance and trust building

### Phase 3: Security and Compliance (1.5 hours)
6. **WHY_SELF_IS_EXTERNALLY_SAFE.md** (30 min)
   - *Why*: External safety guarantees for business confidence
   - *Key Takeaways*: Immutable governance, override prevention, security architecture
   - *Business Impact*: Risk mitigation and customer trust

7. **API_KEY_CREATION_GUIDE.md** (45 min)
   - *Why*: Comprehensive key management for business operations
   - *Key Takeaways*: Key lifecycle, compliance requirements, integration patterns
   - *Business Impact*: Operational security and compliance readiness

8. **SECURITY_VALIDATION_PROCEDURES.md** (45 min)
   - *Why*: Security validation framework for business assurance
   - *Key Takeaways*: Validation procedures, compliance monitoring, audit readiness
   - *Business Impact*: Regulatory compliance and operational excellence

### Phase 4: Assurance and Status (1 hour)
9. **ASSURANCE-BOUNTY.md** (30 min)
   - *Why*: Community-driven safety assurance program
   - *Key Takeaways*: Bug bounty program, community research incentives, collaborative safety
   - *Business Impact*: Community engagement and continuous improvement

10. **ASSURANCE-STATUS.md** (30 min)
   - *Why*: Current program status and transparency metrics
   - *Key Takeaways*: Development timeline, safety validation status
   - *Business Impact*: Assess program maturity and execution risk
