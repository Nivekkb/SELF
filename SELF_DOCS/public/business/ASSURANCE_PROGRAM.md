# Assurance Program (Public)

SELF separates:
1) the **Core license** (MAU-tiered), and
2) optional **Assurance Proof** (procurement-ready evidence).

This document describes the Assurance Program at a high level.

---

## What “Assurance Proof” is

Assurance Proof is a package of evidence and review outputs that helps buyers and stakeholders verify that required safety properties are present in a real deployment.

Assurance Proof is withheld until required properties pass.

---

## What assurance covers (typical scope)

Depending on tier, assurance may include:
- integration review (pre/post flow is implemented correctly)
- validation of required constraints for your use case
- review cadence and governance change control
- incident response expectations and evidence retention
- security/procurement support for enterprise processes

---

## What assurance does NOT cover

Assurance Proof does not replace:
- your product’s escalation paths and disclosures
- clinical or emergency services
- your org’s compliance and security program

SELF is the governance layer; the product still has responsibilities.
