# SELF Stakeholder FAQ

## General Questions

### What is SELF?
SELF (Support-First Logic Engine) is an AI-powered platform designed to provide emotional support, mental health assistance,
 and personal development guidance. It combines advanced AI with human-centered design principles to offer compassionate, safe, and effective support.

### How does SELF differ from other AI mental health apps?
SELF is built with safety as its foundational principle, not an afterthought. It features:
- Constitutional AI architecture with immutable safety rules
- Multi-level human oversight and intervention capabilities
- Transparent governance and comprehensive documentation
- No data monetization or third-party sharing

### Is SELF a replacement for professional therapy?
No. SELF is designed as a supportive tool, not a substitute for professional mental health treatment. It provides general emotional support and can help users identify when they need professional care.

## Safety & Governance

### How safe is SELF?
SELF implements multiple layers of safety measures:
- **Constitutional AI**: Core safety rules that cannot be overridden
- **Real-time monitoring**: Continuous safety assessment during conversations
- **Human oversight**: 24/7 availability of human safety specialists
- **Crisis detection**: Advanced pattern recognition for intervention needs

### What happens if someone is in crisis while using SELF?
SELF has multiple crisis intervention protocols:
- **Immediate detection**: AI pattern recognition for crisis indicators
- **Human intervention**: Real-time connection to trained safety specialists
- **Resource provision**: Direct links to emergency services and crisis hotlines
- **Data preservation**: Appropriate information sharing with emergency services when legally required

### How does SELF handle user data?
- **Data minimization**: Only what is needed for service provision is collected
- **No data sharing**: User data is never sold, traded, or shared with third parties
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **User control**: Complete user control over data retention and deletion

## Privacy & Legal

### What are SELF's data retention policies?
- **Active accounts**: Data retained as long as account is active
- **Inactive accounts**: 3 years of inactivity before deletion
- **Conversations**: Not permanently stored; temporary processing only
- **Legal requirements**: Extended retention only when legally mandated

### Can I delete my SELF account and data?
Yes. Users have the right to:
- **Complete deletion**: Remove account and all associated data
- **Data export**: Download personal data in machine-readable format
- **Partial deletion**: Remove specific data while keeping account active
- **Confirmation**: Receive confirmation when deletion is complete

### What happens to my data if SELF shuts down?
In the unlikely event of service discontinuation:
- **Advance notice**: 90-day notice provided to all users
- **Data export**: Full data export capabilities maintained
- **Deletion option**: Users can request immediate deletion
- **Archival**: Anonymous safety research data may be retained for research purposes

### Does SELF comply with privacy laws?
Yes. SELF complies with:
- **PIPEDA** (Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation) for EU users
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act) for California residents
- **Industry best practices** for AI and mental health data

## Emergency & Crisis

### What should I do in a mental health emergency?
If you're experiencing a mental health emergency:
- **Call emergency services**: 911 (North America) or local emergency number
- **Crisis hotlines**: 988 (US), 9-8-8 (Canada), or local crisis services
- **Seek immediate help**: Don't wait for AI or app-based solutions
- **Tell someone**: Reach out to trusted friends, family, or professionals

### Does SELF provide emergency intervention?
SELF is not an emergency service but includes:
- **Crisis detection**: AI monitoring for crisis indicators
- **Immediate escalation**: Rapid connection to human crisis specialists
- **Resource access**: Direct links to emergency services and hotlines
- **Safety protocols**: Clear procedures for crisis situations

### How does SELF work with emergency services?
When appropriate and legal:
- **Information sharing**: Limited data sharing with emergency services
- **Resource provision**: Direct connection to appropriate emergency contacts
- **Follow-up support**: Post-crisis support and resource connection
- **Privacy respect**: Minimal data sharing with maximum privacy protection
