## What is SELF?

SELF (Support-First Logic Engine) is a groundbreaking AI safety technology that prevents artificial intelligence from making emotional interactions worse. Unlike traditional AI safety that focuses on catastrophic risks, SELF addresses the everyday human cost of AI conversations.

**The Problem**: AI can unintentionally cause emotional distress through poor responses, escalation of sensitive topics, or inability to safely exit conversations.

**The Solution**: SELF acts as an intelligent safety layer that continuously monitors AI conversations and intervenes to protect user emotional well-being.

---

## How SELF Works

### Real-Time Emotional Monitoring
- Analyzes conversation flow and emotional dynamics
- Detects signs of emotional distress or vulnerability
- Evaluates contextual appropriateness of AI responses

### Intelligent Safety Interventions
- **Containment**: Prevents harmful conversation escalation
- **Redirection**: Guides conversations toward safer topics
- **Exit Facilitation**: Ensures users can safely disengage at any time
- **Stabilization**: Helps users reach emotional equilibrium

### Human Oversight Integration
- Complex cases escalate to trained human moderators
- Combines AI efficiency with human empathy and judgment
- Maintains user autonomy while providing safety guardrails
