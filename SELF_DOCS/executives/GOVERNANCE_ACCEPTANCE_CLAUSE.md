## Preamble

By accessing SELF documentation in the executives folder, you ("Executive" or "You") acknowledge and agree to the following governance terms. These terms establish the framework for responsible engagement with SELF's strategic materials, safety documentation, and governance frameworks.

This is not a legal license but a governance agreement that ensures executive access aligns with SELF's mission of safe, ethical, and beneficial AI development.

---

## Acceptance of Governance Principles

### 1. Leadership Safety Commitment
You acknowledge executive responsibility for AI safety and agree to:
- Champion safety-first decision-making in AI initiatives
- Ensure organizational compliance with safety documentation
- Support the development of beneficial AI governance structures
- Lead by example in ethical AI deployment and oversight

### 2. Strategic Responsibility Agreement
You commit to using SELF documentation strategically by:
- Maintaining confidentiality of sensitive strategic information
- Applying insights for organizational benefit and societal good
- Respecting intellectual property and proper attribution
- Ensuring compliance with all applicable governance frameworks

### 3. Ethical Leadership Pledge
You pledge to lead ethically by:
- Making decisions with transparency and accountability
- Avoiding conflicts between profit motives and safety imperatives
- Respecting stakeholder rights and organizational autonomy
- Contributing to industry-wide AI safety advancement

### 4. Documentation Integrity Agreement
You agree to maintain documentation integrity by:
- Not modifying or misrepresenting SELF governance materials
- Properly attributing sources in strategic communications
- Respecting the authority of safety documentation hierarchies
- Following established change management for governance documents

## Access Conditions

### Documentation Access Rights
Subject to your acceptance of these terms, you are granted access to:
- Executive-level strategic documentation and frameworks
- Safety governance and risk management materials
- Business intelligence and market positioning insights
- Leadership guidance on AI safety implementation

### Access Restrictions
You expressly agree not to:
- Share restricted materials with unauthorized personnel
- Use strategic insights for anti-competitive purposes
- Compromise safety documentation integrity
- Distribute materials in violation of governance terms

## Governance Obligations

### Continuous Compliance
You agree to maintain ongoing compliance by:
- Staying informed of governance framework updates
- Participating in executive-level safety governance
- Implementing safety standards in organizational practices
- Supporting industry-wide safety and ethics initiatives

### Reporting Requirements
You commit to reporting:
- Any organizational safety concerns or violations
- Potential risks to AI safety governance frameworks
- Conflicts between business objectives and safety principles
- Material changes requiring governance review

## Executive Accountability

### Leadership Standards
As an executive stakeholder, you agree to:
- Model organizational commitment to AI safety
- Ensure adequate resources for safety implementation
- Foster a culture of ethical AI development
- Maintain transparency with stakeholders and regulators

### Decision-Making Framework
You commit to using SELF governance frameworks by:
- Incorporating safety considerations in strategic decisions
- Consulting safety documentation for risk assessments
- Implementing governance controls in AI initiatives
- Regularly reviewing organizational safety posture

## Dispute Resolution

### Governance Disputes
Any disputes regarding documentation interpretation shall be resolved through:
1. Initial consultation with executive governance representatives
2. Mediation by neutral AI safety and business experts
3. Final determination by the Executive Governance Council

### Termination Rights
Either party may terminate this governance agreement with cause, including:
- Material breach of safety leadership principles
- Violation of strategic responsibility commitments
- Failure to maintain ethical leadership standards
