# SELF™ Is Not For Everyone

## 🚫 Who Should Not Use SELF

**SELF is a safety-critical framework for emotional state management. It requires discipline, resources, and commitment that not all organizations can provide.**

---

## ❌ Organizations That Should Avoid SELF

### 1. Startups Without Safety Resources
**If you:**
- Have no dedicated safety team
- Cannot afford compliance audits
- Lack emotional safety expertise
- Need to "move fast and break things"

**SELF requires:**
- Ongoing safety protocol management
- Regular Doctrine compliance audits
- Dedicated emotional safety personnel
- Conservative development practices

**Alternative:** Build simpler safety layers or use non-emotional AI

---

### 2. Companies Seeking "Easy Safety"
**If you want:**
- "Plug-and-play" emotional safety
- "Set it and forget it" solutions
- Quick implementation without ongoing work
- Minimal compliance overhead

**SELF provides:**
- Rigorous, ongoing safety discipline
- Continuous uncertainty management
- Mandatory audit trails and reporting
- Regular compliance verification

**Alternative:** Use generic chatbot frameworks with appropriate disclaimers

---

### 3. Teams That Can't Handle Uncertainty
**If you:**
- Need binary "safe/unsafe" classifications
- Must assert user safety with confidence
- Can't acknowledge system limitations
- Require clear yes/no answers

**SELF's reality:**
- "At no point does SELF assert user safety"
- All outputs include confidence levels
- Uncertainty is preserved and documented
- False calm detection is prioritized

**Alternative:** Build your own certainty-based system (with appropriate legal advice)

---

### 4. Organizations Resisting Discipline
**If you:**
- Want to modify safety protocols
- Need flexibility in compliance
- Resist uniform safety standards
- Prefer custom safety approaches

**SELF demands:**
- Mandatory Doctrine adherence
- No safety protocol modifications
- Uniform standards across implementations
- Centralized safety governance

**Alternative:** Develop your own safety framework from scratch

---

### 5. Those Unwilling to Be Audited
**If you:**
- Cannot submit to compliance audits
- Won't provide access to implementations
- Resist transparency requirements
- Object to usage monitoring

**SELF requires:**
- Random compliance audits
- Implementation access for verification
- Transparent usage reporting
- Incident cooperation

**Alternative:** Use open-source frameworks without audit requirements

---

## 🎯 Who SELF Is Designed For

**You're a good fit if you:**

✅ **Build safety-critical systems** where emotional safety is paramount
✅ **Prioritize user protection** over competitive advantage
✅ **Embrace structured uncertainty** as a safety feature
✅ **Want defensive infrastructure** with legal protection
✅ **Believe in uniform safety standards** across the industry
✅ **Are willing to be audited** for compliance verification
✅ **Understand emotional safety** requires ongoing discipline

---

## 💡 Responsible Alternatives

**If SELF isn't right for you, consider:**

### 1. Build Your Own Safety Layer
**For:** Organizations with specific safety needs
**Requirements:**
- Develop your own safety protocols
- Implement uncertainty management
- Create audit trails
- Document limitations clearly
**Note:** Don't call it "SELF-compatible" to avoid confusion

### 2. Use Generic Chatbot Frameworks
**For:** Non-emotional use cases
**Options:**
- LangChain with safety modules
- Vercel AI with content moderation
- Custom LLM wrappers
**Requirements:**
- Add appropriate safety disclaimers
- Avoid emotional safety claims
- Implement basic content filtering

### 3. Focus on Non-Emotional Use Cases
**For:** Organizations without safety expertise
**Examples:**
- Customer support for non-sensitive topics
- Information retrieval systems
- Productivity assistants
- Entertainment chatbots

### 4. Partner with Licensed Providers
**For:** Organizations that need safety without expertise
**Benefits:**
- Leverage provider's safety expertise
- Reduce your compliance burden
- Access pre-built safety solutions
**Requirements:**
- Ensure partner uses SELF responsibly
- Maintain transparency with users

---

## ⚠️ Final Warning

**SELF is not:**
- A shortcut to emotional safety compliance
- A way to avoid legal responsibility
- A substitute for human judgment
- A flexible framework you can adapt
- A guarantee against all risks

**SELF is:**
- A disciplined approach to uncertainty management
- A framework that forces you to acknowledge limitations
- A system that puts user safety above all else
- A license that can be terminated for violations
- A commitment to ongoing safety evolution

**If this doesn't align with your capabilities or values, please don't use SELF.**

**We exist to prevent emotional safety failures—not to enable them.**

**For everyone else: We wish you success, but please build responsibly.**
