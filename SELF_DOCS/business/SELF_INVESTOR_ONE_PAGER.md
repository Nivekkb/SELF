## What it is
**SELF** is a safety + governance + assurance layer that helps AI systems **avoid making emotional interactions worse**. It is designed for emotionally charged AI surfaces (support, coaching, education, community, care navigation) where reliability under distress matters.

## The problem
Most “AI safety” is implemented as fragile prompt rules that are hard to test and impossible to audit. In real deployments, failure modes look like:
- escalation of distress, invalidation, shame
- coercion / dependency loops / boundary violations
- unsafe crisis handling
- un-defendable claims (“we’re safe”) without evidence

## The solution (how SELF works)
SELF adds an enforceable layer between user ↔ model:
- **State-aware constraints** (risk-sensitive behavior under emotional load)
- **Repair/enforcement** logic that rewrites/blocks unsafe model outputs
- **Governance + change control** tied to releases (not just policy PDFs)
- **Audit events** to make safety decisions observable and monitorable
- **Assurance artifacts** (failure modes, guarantee matrix, threat model)

## Why SELF wins (sharp)
- **Reliability under emotional load:** behavior is constrained by state + enforcement, not “hope the model complies.”
- **Governance as operations:** safety becomes **testable, monitorable, explainable**; not paperwork.
- **Assurance as a product:** customers can obtain proof outputs to support procurement and defensible claims.
- **Integration-first SDK:** model-agnostic layer that fits existing stacks; avoids vendor lock-in.

## Customers / use cases
Initial beachheads (high-trust, high-liability):
- AI customer support and complaint resolution
- coaching/wellness (non-clinical) and youth-facing education
- community/group facilitation and moderation assistants

Buyer personas: Product, Trust & Safety/Risk, Security/Privacy, Legal/Compliance.

## Business model (pricing logic)
SELF is an SDK. Pricing separates **license** from **proof**:
- **SELF Core license:** MAU-tiered (adoption/impact aligned) — Starter $300/mo (≤5k MAU), Growth $750/mo (≤25k), Scale $2,000/mo (≤100k), Enterprise annual (starts $36k/yr)
- **Assurance Proof add-on:** priced by risk + review cadence (procurement-ready evidence)
- **Enterprise contracts:** annual terms (SLA, security review, procurement support)
- **Usage billing:** only for optional managed add-ons (e.g., managed evaluation runs)
