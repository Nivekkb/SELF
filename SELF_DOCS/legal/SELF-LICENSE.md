# SELF™ LICENSING

**Licensing Philosophy and Implementation**
*(Policy – Version 1.0)*

---

## Core Licensing Principle

SELF's licensing exists to protect users, not to restrict access.

Licensing decisions serve safety and accountability, not revenue maximization.

---

## Dual License Structure

SELF operates under a dual license model:

1. **Open Access for Safety Research**: Academic and safety research institutions may use SELF for research purposes under terms that preserve safety integrity.

2. **Commercial License for Production Use**: Any production deployment requires commercial licensing to ensure responsible implementation and safety compliance.

---

## Safety as Licensing Condition

All SELF licenses include mandatory safety obligations:

- Strict adherence to the Design Doctrine
- Implementation of all safety protocols
- Preservation of uncertainty acknowledgments
- Regular safety audits and reporting
- Immediate reporting of safety incidents

Licenses are revocable for safety violations.

---

## Commercial License Requirements

Production use requires commercial licensing because:

- Safety compliance demands resources and expertise
- Implementation quality affects user outcomes
- Support and updates require sustainable funding
- Liability considerations require formal agreements
- Safety monitoring requires dedicated infrastructure

Commercial licensing funds the maintenance of safety standards.

---

## Open Access for Research

Research use is permitted under these conditions:

- Research must advance emotional AI safety
- Findings must be published and shared
- Safety protocols must be preserved
- Commercialization requires separate licensing
- Attribution must be maintained

Research access enables safety improvements for all users.

---

## License Enforcement

Licensing terms are enforced through:

- Technical measures (API keys, version control)
- Legal agreements with audit rights
- Community monitoring and reporting
- Automatic revocation for violations

Enforcement protects the integrity of the safety system.

---

## No Perpetual Licenses

No SELF license is perpetual:

- All licenses have defined terms
- Renewal requires safety compliance review
- Technology evolution may require license updates
- Safety incidents trigger license suspension

Perpetual licenses create complacency; time-limited licenses maintain vigilance.

---

## Geographic and Jurisdictional Considerations

SELF licensing considers:

- Local laws regarding AI safety and liability
- Data protection and privacy regulations
- Crisis response infrastructure availability
- Cultural contexts for emotional support

Licensing adapts to responsible deployment requirements.

---

## License Pricing Philosophy

Commercial license pricing reflects:

- Implementation complexity and safety requirements
- Support and monitoring costs
- Liability and insurance considerations
- Market capacity to pay for safety
- Subsidy for safety research and development

Pricing serves safety sustainability, not profit maximization.

---

## Final Licensing Statement

SELF licensing exists because safety has costs that must be borne by those who benefit from it.

Licensing ensures that safety is not treated as a commodity to be minimized, but as a responsibility to be honored.

The goal of SELF licensing is not to exclude, but to ensure that inclusion happens safely and responsibly.
